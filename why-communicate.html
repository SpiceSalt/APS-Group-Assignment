Why to communicate?

Communication- An Overlooked Science

  • Oftentimes scientists can feel like it's not worth it because the general public will never understand- that's not true!
  • These reasons are why we created this page. Trust us, it will be easy and simple to learn how to communicate with other scientists, the general public, and anyone after this page
  • Feel free to browse it based on what specific struggles you're having in communication

Bridging the Gap

  • It's important for 'regular' people who aren't scientists and to understand the ideas that scientists have
  • If we don't have any way to communicate with the general public, there will be a disconnection between scientists & the rest of the world
  • Bridging the 'knowledge gap' allows

Funding your Dreams

  • If you can't communicate, you likely won't be able to convince others on why your scientific work matters
  • Politicians specifically may not be convinced if you lack the ability to communicate why your idea is worth funding.
