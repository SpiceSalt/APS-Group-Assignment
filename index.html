Welcome to the Science Communication Platform!

This is the Science Communication Platform of the Faculty of Science. We cater to individuals from various scientific academic backgrounds, from students to researchers.

As part of our platform, we are organizing a series of events around various aspects of science communication, which should be seen as supporting your courses or research.
